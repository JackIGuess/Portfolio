      About Me

      My name is Jacques Gwenin, and I am a final-year Games Design student at Staffordshire University. I have a deep passion for combat systems, environments, and level design, especially using Unreal Engine 5.

      Inspired by my love for fantasy and the outdoors, I channel creativity by exaggerating real-world elements to craft immersive, captivating worlds that invite players to explore and experience something truly unique, whether this be through programming, level, or environmental design.

      Driven by a strong work ethic and a collaborative spirit, I thrive in team environments where clear communication and professional collaboration fuel innovation. I am committed to continuously pushing my creative boundaries, sharpening my skills, and building meaningful relationships to deliver outstanding projects.
